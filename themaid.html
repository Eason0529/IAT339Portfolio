Narrative & Interactive web game

Roles:

Story Narrator, Web Designer

Tools:

Wix system, Photoshop and Illustrator

Project Overview:

The Maid is a web based interactive game. Player will explore the story from the perspective of "Doris". During the process of the game, player can interact with the elements appear on the screen and try to collect information and clue to progress the story forward. The player starts the protagonist Doris a new day by clicking the “Start” button. Meanwhile, the player's mission is to help Doris figure out what is happening behind the peaceful daily life then try to save herself. Our game has three suspects totally, and the player as Doris need to investigate the house through and collect the information as much as they can to find out the bad guy.

Before the player chooses any suspects, the game will gradually explain each character and their relationship with the protagonist by investigating the clues in the room. For example, when the player enters Denny’s room, they could open Denny’s diary on the table to understand Denny recently fall in love in school. When they come to Marcus’s laboratory, the player can discover Marcus’s research paper to realize he is now studying on the clone.

After gathering these clues, the game will keep moving on, and the player will help Doris to decide how she needs to react for the next step. Player’s decision will determine the story’s ending differently and affect Doris’s destiny. This game has two ends. If the player, choose the correct suspect she will protect herself correctly and keeps herself alive. However, in other words, wrong suspect selection will cause her death.

Role Description

Doris (Maid) 38-year-old. Doris has a miserable childhood. She was once saved by the manor owner Schultz • Hoffmann. After that, she has worked in the manor as a maid for 26 years. Being grateful to milord, Doris dedicated her heart to keep the manor organized and beautiful, during these years, she already thinks of this place as her home. Doris has a strong OCD and would like to keep everything in order. She began to suspect and decided to investigate when she feels something wrong among the family in this manner.

Marcus • Hoffmann 46-year-old scientist. Son of Schultz • Hoffmann. Marcus’s research direction is to clone people. He was a generous person, a perfect husband, and a kind father until he finds out that Daniel is not his son, instead, was given birth by Marina and another man. He became furious and lost all his reason. Finally, he used Daniel to test for the clone experiment and killed the real Daniel to keep this secret.

Donna Marina • Hoffmann 39-year-old artist, wife of Marcus • Hoffmann, sculptor. Marina has always been the perfect wife, beautiful, elegant, patient and cares about her son. After her husband Marcus found out the truth and ruined all of this, she decided to revenge for Daniel. As a perfect woman, she sure would not allow anyone aware of what she did. So the smart and suspicious maid become her next target.

Daniel • Hoffmann 13 years old, Son of Marcus and Marina. An intelligent, polite, innocent young man.

Process

As a maid, she(the player) can find out the ending based on her understanding by discovering a series of clues throughout the manor: Marcus is a scientist whose researching the cloning technology. Marcus found that Daniel is not the son of him, so he used Daniel as his experimental object. After murdered the real Daniel, the cloned “Daniel” has already replaced the real Daniel. After Donna Marina found out this, to revenge for her own son, she cloned Marcus through the machine and then made the real Marcus into a sculpture. Marina was afraid of the truth being exposed as the maid(the player) has started to investigate, so she decided to clone Doris and then kill her.

This is a game that requires players to prevent themselves from being killed while solving crimes. If the player(Doris) can inference all the cases, then he/she can avoid being murdered. If not, the player (Doris) would be killed by Marina at the end.

In order to allow us to feel the progress of the game more intuitively and clearly, we created a table to hold the appearance of all pages. With this table, the options and branches of the game are clear. For the concept art we made two scenes to show the preview of the game. The first is the place our story happened--Palmer Manor, the maid and Hoffman family lives here. The second is Marcus's laboratory with the clone machines. For the storyboards, we got a hierarchy chart to show the flow of the game and three storyboards showing details about what's going on and what the player would see in each scene, also including the stuffs can be investigated and how different choices would effect and lead the game to different endings.

At the same time, because the task requirements do not allow us to use too much material on the Internet, we spend a lot of time collecting materials from the Internet and post-processing these pictures through such as illustrator, Photoshop and even Maya. For example, for the pictures I listed here, we download free models of Maya and post-render them. After that, we found landscape pictures that fit our game style. Finally, we put them together by software. All the pictures you can see on the webpage are finished by our group one by one.
